about me

I am Tiago Martins, a junior data analyst, motivated by the belief that data can be used to make better decisions.

I am motivated by working with organizations that want to make effective use of their data. I pretend to analyze, manage and present data through dynamic reports in order to help companies achieve their main goals.

I have knowledge in EXCEL, SQL and at the moment I am creating reports in POWER BI that you can visualize in the section below.

I pretend to work for a company with well-defined goals and work in an exciting team environment.
